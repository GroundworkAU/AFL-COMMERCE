Returns & exchanges

Australian Consumer Law friendly

We want you in the right gear. Return unworn items with tags within 30 days for a refund or exchange.

What you can return
Unworn, unwashed items with tags attached, in their original packaging.
Personalised items
Guernseys with a name or number cannot be returned unless faulty.
How to start
Reply to your order email, or contact the club store with your order number.
Faulty items
Always covered under Australian Consumer Law, whenever you bought them.
